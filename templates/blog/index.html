Welcome to Rick Will Fix It.

Community Bugs

Rick Sanchez will try to address any issues or bugs you may currently have, as long as it serves his own purposes also. Our developer Meeseeks will attend to most bugs. Please search through current bugs before posting a new one. Our community members can upvote a particular bug. You must register before you can post a new bug. Upvotes will determine time given to solving each bug.

Feature Requests and Curse Purge Plus

If you would like Rick to create a new product or feature for you, you will have to pay. If you can't afford it there's a good chance our many patrons will contribute to your cause. You can submit a request for a new feature and once it has been priced, contributions can be made. Mr Sanchez pledges to spend 50% of his time working on the highest paid feature.

Curse Purge Plus services will also be available through this system.

You must register an account before proposing a new feature.
